The key focus area for Genpact right now is LEAN Digital. As the name suggests, this website has been exclusively created with the purpose of improving our Digital Footprint. It has The key to our success is collaboration, and all are requested to come forward and make this initiative a success.

One of our big focus areas in this year is LEAN Digital. We are creating a forum to improve our Digital Footprint.
In this forum all the team members from Taranvir's teams will be present.
